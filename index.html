The Kit

The Caltech LIGO Lab EPO Kit is always evolving, but may include materials such as:
  • A model interferometer
  • Slinkies for wave demos
  • AV equipment for videos

The Procedure

  • Ask Alan or Jonah to add your event to the sign-up calendar
  • On the day of your event, get the kit from room XXX. If needed, ask Alan or Nicole Washington for the key.
  • After the event, return the kit to room XXX.
  • Add notes and pictures about your event to the EPO Wiki
